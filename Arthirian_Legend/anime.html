Fate

The Fate series is surprisingly close to the Arthurian Legend. It is surprising because Arthur in Fate is a woman who pretended to be a man during her life. Other than some of the knights also being genderbent and Excailiber being a two handed weapon it gets the details correct. Though if it ever shows Arthur's Britin it is in a flash back. In Fate heroes are summend by mages to fight in the modern world for the Holy Grail. The mages and the summend heroes have a wish they want granted. Things in Fate can get confusing quickly though the back stories of the summend heros is mostly accuret to their real world legends.

The King Arthur from Fate is one of the closest depictions of the Arthurian Legends made recently. Which is sad considering Arthur in the Fate series is a woman. That should make Fate the lesest acuret, but the creator got the other things right. The creator of Fate thought about doing a male Arthur but changed his mind in pre-production for some reason.

Seven Deadly Sins

This version of Arthur is young just starting his rule and not a main character. He is more of an important secondary character. The anime The Seven Deadly Sins focuses on characters that didn't play a big part in the Arthurian Legend. When Arthur first aperars in the show it is just so Merlin can be part of the main cast. In a later secion of the show Arthur has more to do. Namely he aquires Excailiber which was a litle odd. In this anime Arthur was already king before he pulled the sword from the stone. The Sword in the Stone and Excailiber were also combined into the same sword. Which dose seem to be a comin mistake people seem to make. It will be interesting to see what The Seven Deadly Sins will do with Arthur after were the show had him at the end of its most recent sesion.

The show is a fun watch if odd at times. With it spending time away from Arthur and focusing on undermentioned characters there is less to compair it to. Though when The magor players from the Arthurin Legend do show up it shows the libertyies taken with the sorce material, because of how unknown most of the characters used are.
